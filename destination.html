Manali Highlights

Solang Valley

A popular tourist destination near Manali in Himachal Pradesh, is known for its stunning natural beauty, adventure activities, and is a year-round destination offering both winter sports and summer activities.

Atal Tunnel

The Border Roads Organisation (BRO) is a road construction executive force in India that provides support to Indian Armed Forces.

Bhrigu Lake

A high-altitude glacial lake in the Kullu district of Himachal Pradesh, India, is renowned for its stunning beauty and is considered a popular trek for both novice and experienced trekkers, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding Himalayan peaks.
