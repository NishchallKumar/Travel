Bali Highlights

Ubud Rice Terraces

Walk through the breathtaking Tegallalang Rice Terraces, a stunning example of Bali's subak irrigation system and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Uluwatu Temple

Perched on a steep cliff 70 meters above sea level, this sea temple offers spectacular sunset views and traditional Kecak dance performances.

Nusa Penida

Take a boat trip to this nearby island paradise featuring dramatic cliffs, crystal clear waters, and some of Bali's best snorkeling spots.
