The Himalayan Paradise

Nestled in the mountains of Himachal Pradesh, Manali is a year-round destination offering breathtaking landscapes and thrilling adventures.

Natural Beauty

Manali is surrounded by snow-capped peaks, lush green valleys, apple orchards, and gushing rivers, creating a picture-perfect setting.

Adventure Hub

From skiing and snowboarding in winter to paragliding, rafting, and trekking in summer, Manali offers endless adventure opportunities.

Cultural Heritage

Explore ancient temples, traditional Himachali architecture, and experience the warm hospitality of the local people.

Travel Tips

Make the most of your Himalayan adventure

Best Time to Visit

March-June: Pleasant weather (10°C-25°C), ideal for adventure sports and sightseeing.
October-February: For snowfall and winter sports (temperatures can drop to -7°C).
July-September: Monsoon season with landslides - travel with caution.

Getting There

Air: Kullu-Manali Airport (KUU) 50km from Manali
Road: Well-connected by road from Delhi (540km), Chandigarh (310km)
Train: Nearest major station is Chandigarh (310km)

Adventure Activities

Winter: Skiing, snowboarding, snowmobiling at Solang and Rohtang
Summer: Paragliding, river rafting, zorbing, trekking
Year-round: Mountain biking, camping, rock climbing

Essential Packing

Warm clothing (even in summer), comfortable trekking shoes, sunscreen, sunglasses, medicines for altitude sickness, power bank (for remote areas).
